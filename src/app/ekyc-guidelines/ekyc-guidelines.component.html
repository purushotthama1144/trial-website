Alice Blue Account Opening - Detailed Step-by-Step Guide

STEP 1: Registration
  • Visit Alice Blue Ekyc Portal and fill in your basic information.
  • Click on ‘Open an Account’ if you are registering for the first time or click on ‘Continue Application’.
STEP 2: Verify PAN Details
  • Enter your PAN Number and Date of Birth.
STEP 3: Contact Information
  • In this step, your aadhar details and address will be fetched automatically.
  • If your Aadhar and Correspondence Address is different, you can add the Correspondence Address in the ‘Address Line 2’.
STEP 4: Bank Details
  • Enter your Bank Account Number, IFSC Code (Attach 3 Month Bank Statement if you want to activate F&O and MCX Segment).
  • Attach cancelled cheque leaf (Only accepted in PNG and JPG Format).
STEP 5: Personal and Financial Details
  • In this step add your Personal and Financial details like Fathers Name, Mothers Name, Education, Annual Income, Networth, etc.
  • If you wish to add a nominee, click ‘Yes’ and add in the nominee details.
STEP 6: Signature Verification
  • Sign on a white paper and upload the image.
  • Select your demat preference: Select ‘POA’ if you can sign and send a physical copy of POA Form or Select ‘E-DIS’.
  • Select ‘Brokerage Plan’ and click enter.
STEP 7: Enable Cookies
  • Enable cookies (Follow the steps mentioned in the pop up)
STEP 8: Preview your Information
  • Recheck if the information provided is correct.
STEP 9: Upload Photo and Select E-sign Process
  • Take a live photo, upload it and select from Aadhar or Pan E-sign Process.
STEP 10: E-sign
  • Enter your Aadhar Number, Verify your Aadhar with OTP and download the E-sign pdf for your reference.
  • Your account will be activated within 24 hours and you will receive the login id and password to your registered email address.
